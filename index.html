A bit about me

Hey, I'm Devgupta — a backend developer who genuinely enjoys the part of engineering most people skip over: understanding why things work, not just how to make them work.

I've spent years building APIs, designing data pipelines, debugging race conditions at 2am, and writing about all of it. I believe the best engineers are the ones who can explain a complex system to a junior dev and to a non-technical founder in the same breath.

When I'm not writing code, I'm writing about code — my AI-powered dev blog publishes a new technical post every single day, covering real lessons from real production systems.

I'm currently open to backend / fullstack roles where I can build systems that actually matter.
